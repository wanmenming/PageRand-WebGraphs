</p><p>A Climate Action Plan (CAP) is a set of strategies intended to guide efforts for <a href="/wiki/Climate_change_mitigation" title="Climate change mitigation">climate change mitigation</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Bierbaum_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Bierbaum-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
