<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Urban reforestation</h1>			<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
				<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>				<div id="contentSub"></div>
				<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p class="mw-empty-elt">
</p><p><b>Urban reforestation</b> is the practice of planting trees, typically on a large scale, in <a href="/wiki/Urban_environment" class="mw-redirect" title="Urban environment">urban environments</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Shading_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Shading-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It sometimes includes also <a href="/wiki/Urban_horticulture" title="Urban horticulture">urban horticulture</a> and <a href="/wiki/Urban_farming" class="mw-redirect" title="Urban farming">urban farming</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Australia_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Australia-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Reasons for practicing urban reforestation include urban <a href="/wiki/Beautification" title="Beautification">beautification</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-Shading_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Shading-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> increasing shade,<sup id="cite_ref-Shading_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Shading-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> modifying the <a href="/wiki/Urban_climate" title="Urban climate">urban climate</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-Manchester_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Manchester-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> improving <a href="/wiki/Air_pollution" title="Air pollution">air quality</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and restoration of <a href="/wiki/Urban_forest" title="Urban forest">urban forests</a> after a <a href="/wiki/Natural_disaster" title="Natural disaster">natural disaster</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-storms_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-storms-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Programs">Programs</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Urban_reforestation&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Programs">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Large scale urban reforestation programs include <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Million_Tree_Initiative" title="Million Tree Initiative">Million Tree Initiative</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-Queens_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Queens-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/TreePeople" title="TreePeople">TreePeople</a> in <a href="/wiki/Los_Angeles" title="Los Angeles">Los Angeles</a>, which planted 1 million trees in preparation for the <a href="/wiki/1984_Summer_Olympics" title="1984 Summer Olympics">1984 Summer Olympics</a> and continued planting thereafter.<sup id="cite_ref-Shading_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Shading-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Grassroots efforts include <a href="/wiki/Friends_of_the_Urban_Forest" title="Friends of the Urban Forest">Friends of the Urban Forest</a> in <a href="/wiki/San_Francisco" title="San Francisco">San Francisco</a> which advocates the planting of street trees<sup id="cite_ref-Shading_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Shading-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and the <i>Urban Reforestation</i> organization in Australia, which focuses on <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_living" title="Sustainable living">sustainable living</a> in urban places.<sup id="cite_ref-Australia_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Australia-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Criticisms">Criticisms</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Urban_reforestation&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Criticisms">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Urban reforestation efforts compete for money and urban land that could be used for other purposes. For example, effort placed in planting new trees can take away from maintenance of already established trees.<sup id="cite_ref-Queens_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Queens-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
