<p><b>Promethium(III) oxide</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Chemical_compound" title="Chemical compound">compound</a> with the formula Pm<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub>. It is the most common form of <a href="/wiki/Promethium" title="Promethium">promethium</a>.
</p>
