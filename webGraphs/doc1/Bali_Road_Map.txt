<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Bali Road Map</h1>			<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
				<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>				<div id="contentSub"></div>
				<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>After the <a href="/wiki/2007_United_Nations_Climate_Change_Conference" title="2007 United Nations Climate Change Conference">2007 United Nations Climate Change Conference</a> on the island <a href="/wiki/Bali" title="Bali">Bali</a> in <a href="/wiki/Indonesia" title="Indonesia">Indonesia</a> in December, 2007 the participating nations adopted the <b>Bali Road Map</b> as a two-year process to finalizing a binding agreement in <a href="/wiki/2009_United_Nations_Climate_Change_Conference" title="2009 United Nations Climate Change Conference">2009 in Copenhagen</a>. The conference encompassed meetings of several bodies, including the 13th Conference of the Parties to the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (COP 13) and the 3rd Meeting of the Parties to the Kyoto Protocol (MOP 3 or CMP 3).
</p><p>The Bali Road Map includes the Bali Action Plan (BAP) that was adopted by Decision 1/CP.13 of the <a href="/wiki/COP-13" class="mw-redirect" title="COP-13">COP-13</a>. It also includes the Ad Hoc Working Group on Further Commitments for Annex I Parties under the Kyoto Protocol (AWG-KP)<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> negotiations and their 2009 deadline, the launch of the Adaptation Fund, the scope and content of the Article 9 review of the <a href="/wiki/Kyoto_Protocol" title="Kyoto Protocol">Kyoto Protocol</a>, as well as decisions on technology transfer and on reducing emissions from deforestation.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Bali_Action_Plan">Bali Action Plan</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bali_Road_Map&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Bali Action Plan">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Pillars">Pillars</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bali_Road_Map&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Pillars">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Conference of Parties decided to launch a comprehensive process to enable the implementation of the Convention through long-term cooperative action, now, up to and beyond 2012, by addressing: (the called pillars or building blocks)
</p>
<ul><li>A shared vision for long-term cooperative action, including a long-term global goal for <a href="/wiki/Greenhouse_gas#Greenhouse_gas_emissions" title="Greenhouse gas">emission reductions</a>.</li>
<li>Enhanced national/international action on <a href="/wiki/Mitigation_of_climate_change" class="mw-redirect" title="Mitigation of climate change">mitigation of climate change</a>.</li>
<li>Enhanced action on adaptation.</li>
<li>Enhanced action on <a href="/wiki/Technology_development" class="mw-redirect" title="Technology development">technology development</a> and transfer to support action on mitigation and adaptation.</li>
<li>Enhanced action on the provision of financial resources and investment to support action on mitigation and adaptation and technology cooperation.</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Cutting_emissions">Cutting emissions</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bali_Road_Map&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Cutting emissions">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The nations acknowledge that evidence for <a href="/wiki/Global_warming" title="Global warming">global warming</a> is <i>unequivocal</i>, and that humans must reduce emissions to reduce the risks of "severe climate change impacts" and emphasized the urgency to address climate change. There was a strong consensus for updated changes for both developed and developing countries. Although there were not specific numbers agreed upon in order to cut emissions,the decision recognized that there was a need for "deep cuts in global emissions" (plural countries proposed 100% reduction in 2050) and that "developed country emissions must fall 10-40% by 2020".<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Mitigation">Mitigation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bali_Road_Map&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Mitigation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Enhanced action on mitigation of climate change includes, inter alia:
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Nationally_Appropriate_Mitigation_Action" title="Nationally Appropriate Mitigation Action">Nationally appropriate mitigation commitments</a> or actions by all developed countries.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Nationally_Appropriate_Mitigation_Action" title="Nationally Appropriate Mitigation Action">Nationally appropriate mitigation actions (NAMAs)</a> by developing countries.</li>
<li>Cooperative sectorial approaches and sector-specific actions (CSAs).</li>
<li>Ways to strengthen the catalytic role of the convention.</li></ul>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Forests">Forests</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bali_Road_Map&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Forests">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/REDD-plus" class="mw-redirect" title="REDD-plus">REDD-plus</a></div>
<p>The nations pledge "<a href="/wiki/Policy" title="Policy">policy</a> <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/approach" class="extiw" title="wikt:approach">approaches</a> and positive incentives" on issues relating to <a href="/wiki/Reducing_emissions_from_deforestation_and_forest_degradation" title="Reducing emissions from deforestation and forest degradation">reducing emissions from deforestation and forest degradation</a> (<a href="/wiki/REDD" class="mw-redirect" title="REDD">REDD</a>) in developing countries; and enhancement of <a href="/wiki/Forest_carbon_stock" class="mw-redirect" title="Forest carbon stock">forest carbon stock</a> in developing countries This paragraph is referred to as “REDD-plus”.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Adaptation">Adaptation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bali_Road_Map&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Adaptation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The nations opt for enhanced co-operation to "support urgent implementation" of measures to protect poorer countries against climate change, including NAPAs. impacts.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Technology">Technology</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bali_Road_Map&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Technology">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In <a href="/wiki/Technology_development" class="mw-redirect" title="Technology development">technology development</a> and <a href="/wiki/Technology_transfer" title="Technology transfer">transfer</a>, the nations will consider how to facilitate the transfer of clean and renewable energy technologies from industrialised nations to the developing countries.This includes, inter alia:
</p>
<ul><li>Removal of obstacles to, and provision of financial and other incentives for, <a href="/wiki/Scalability" title="Scalability">scaling up</a> the development and transfer of technology to developing country Parties in order to promote access to affordable <a href="/wiki/Environmentally_sound_technology" class="mw-redirect" title="Environmentally sound technology">environmentally sound technologies</a> (renewable energies, electric vehicles).</li>
<li>Ways to accelerate the <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/deployment" class="extiw" title="wikt:deployment">deployment</a>, <a href="https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/diffussion" class="extiw" title="wikt:diffussion">diffussion</a> and transfer of such technologies.</li>
<li>Cooperation on research and development of current, new and innovative technology, including <a href="/wiki/Win-win_solution" class="mw-redirect" title="Win-win solution">win-win solutions</a>.</li>
<li>The effectiveness of mechanism and tools for technology cooperation in specific sectors.</li></ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Finance">Finance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bali_Road_Map&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Finance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Provision of financial resources and investment includes:
</p>
<ul><li>Improved access to predictable and sustainable financial resources and the provision of new and additional resources, including <a href="/wiki/Official_funding" class="mw-redirect" title="Official funding">official</a> and <a href="/wiki/Concessional_funding" class="mw-redirect" title="Concessional funding">concessional funding</a> for developing country Parties (dcP).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Positive_incentive" class="mw-redirect" title="Positive incentive">Positive incentives</a> for dcP for national mitigation strategies and adaptation action.</li>
<li>Innovative means of funding for dcP that are particularly vulnerable to the <a href="/wiki/Adverse_impacts_of_climate_change" class="mw-redirect" title="Adverse impacts of climate change">adverse impacts of climate change</a> in meeting the costs of adaptation.</li>
<li>Incentivisation of adaptation actions on the basis of <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_development" title="Sustainable development">sustainable development</a> policies.</li>
<li>Mobilization of funding and investment, including facilitation of <a href="/wiki/Climate-friendly_investment" class="mw-redirect" title="Climate-friendly investment">climate-friendly investment</a> choices.</li>
<li>Financial and technical support for <a href="/wiki/Capacity-building" class="mw-redirect" title="Capacity-building">capacity-building</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Assessment_of_cost" class="mw-redirect" title="Assessment of cost">assessment of costs</a> of adaptation in developing countries, to aid in determining their financial needs.</li></ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Ad_Hoc_Working_Groups">Ad Hoc Working Groups</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bali_Road_Map&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Ad Hoc Working Groups">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Conference decided to establish two subsidiary bodies under the Convention to conduct the process, the <a href="/wiki/Ad_Hoc_Working_Group_on_Long-term_Cooperative_Action" title="Ad Hoc Working Group on Long-term Cooperative Action">Ad Hoc Working Group on Long-term Cooperative Action</a> (AWG-LCA) and the <a href="/wiki/Ad_Hoc_Working_Group_on_Further_Commitments_for_Annex_I_Parties_under_the_Kyoto_Protocol" title="Ad Hoc Working Group on Further Commitments for Annex I Parties under the Kyoto Protocol">Ad Hoc Working Group on Further Commitments for Annex I Parties under the Kyoto Protocol</a> (AWG-KP), that were to complete their work in 2009 and present the outcome to the <a href="/wiki/COP15" class="mw-redirect" title="COP15">COP15</a>/MOP 5.
</p><p>The AWG-LCA and AWG-KP presented draft conclusions to <a href="/wiki/COP15" class="mw-redirect" title="COP15">COP15 and CMP5</a>, which contained many unresolved issues. The working groups were subsequently asked to report to <a href="/wiki/COP16" class="mw-redirect" title="COP16">COP16 and CMP6</a> in Cancun, Mexico.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Timescales">Timescales</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Bali_Road_Map&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Timescales">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Four major UNFCCC meetings to implement the Bali Road Map were planned for 2008, with the first to be held in either March or April and the second in June, with the third in either August or September followed by a major meeting in <a href="/wiki/Pozna%C5%84" title="Poznań">Poznań</a>, Poland in December 2008. The negotiations process was scheduled to conclude at the <a href="/wiki/United_Nations_Climate_Change_Conference_2009" class="mw-redirect" title="United Nations Climate Change Conference 2009">United Nations Climate Change Conference 2009</a> in Copenhagen, Denmark.
</p>
