<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Sustainability studies</h1>			<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
				<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>				<div id="contentSub"></div>
				<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b>Sustainability studies</b> focus on the interdisciplinary perspective of the <a href="/wiki/Sustainability" title="Sustainability">sustainability</a> concept. Programs include instruction in <a href="/wiki/Sustainable_development" title="Sustainable development">sustainable development</a>, <a href="/wiki/Geography" title="Geography">geography</a>, <a href="/wiki/Environmental_policy" title="Environmental policy">environmental policies</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ethics" title="Ethics">ethics</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ecology" title="Ecology">ecology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Landscape_architecture" title="Landscape architecture">landscape architecture</a>, <a href="/wiki/City_planning" class="mw-redirect" title="City planning">city</a> and <a href="/wiki/Regional_planning" title="Regional planning">regional planning</a>, <a href="/wiki/Economics" title="Economics">economics</a>, <a href="/wiki/Natural_resources" class="mw-redirect" title="Natural resources">natural resources</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sociology" title="Sociology">sociology</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Anthropology" title="Anthropology">anthropology</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Sustainability studies also focuses on the importance of climate change, poverty and development. <sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Studies in Sustainability are now available in many different universities across America. The main goal of sustainability studies is for students to find ways to develop creative solutions to the crisis in environmental sustainability. <sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
