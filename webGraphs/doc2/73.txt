<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Green Party of Canada leadership elections</h1>			<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
				<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>				<div id="contentSub"></div>
				<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>As stipulated under the party's current constitution, the <b><a href="/wiki/Green_Party_of_Canada" title="Green Party of Canada">Green Party of Canada</a></b> holds a national leadership election every 4 years.
</p><p>Ballots are always mailed out in advance to all Green Party of Canada "members in good standing" - allowing the option of voting by mail to all party members who do not wish to attend the convention in person.
</p><p>The party uses an <a href="/wiki/Instant-runoff_voting" title="Instant-runoff voting">instant-runoff voting (IRV)</a> ballot system for the election of its leader and councillors and a standard yes-or-no ballot for voting on constitutional amendments.
</p>
