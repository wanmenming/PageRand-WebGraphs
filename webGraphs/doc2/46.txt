<p>The <b>Green Party</b> (<a href="/wiki/Romanian_language" title="Romanian language">Romanian</a>: <i lang="ro">Partidul Verde</i>), often shortened to <b>The Greens</b> (<i lang="ro" title="Romanian language text">Verzii</i>) is a Romanian political party centred on <a href="/wiki/Green_politics" title="Green politics">green politics</a>. It is a member of the <a href="/wiki/European_Green_Party" title="European Green Party">European Green Party</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Policies">Policies</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Green_Party_(Romania)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Policies">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Despite its support for environmental causes, such as reforestation and reductions in carbon emissions, the Green Party also advocates some free-market policies, such as the <a href="/wiki/Privatisation" class="mw-redirect" title="Privatisation">privatisation</a> of state-owned enterprises. It supports an extension in individual rights as well as greater <a href="/wiki/Separation_of_church_and_state" title="Separation of church and state">separation of church and state</a>, including a gradual removal of all state funding for religious institutions.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (November 2008)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> Like the vast majority of Romanian political parties, it is supportive of <a href="/wiki/European_integration" title="European integration">European integration</a>. It is the Romanian party that is most supportive of <a href="/wiki/LGBT_rights_in_Romania" title="LGBT rights in Romania">LGBT rights</a>, supporting same-sex marriage and civil partnerships, as well as measures to reduce homophobia in schools and society at large.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
