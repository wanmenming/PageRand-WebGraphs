<p>The <b>Yukon Green Party</b> is a territorial green political party in <a href="/wiki/Yukon" title="Yukon">Yukon</a>, Canada. It was inspired by the <a href="/wiki/Green_Party_of_Canada" title="Green Party of Canada">Green Party of Canada</a>. Its interim leader is <a href="/wiki/Frank_de_Jong" title="Frank de Jong">Frank de Jong</a>, a school teacher in <a href="/wiki/Faro,_Yukon" title="Faro, Yukon">Faro, Yukon</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-WDSdj_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WDSdj-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Its first leader was Kristina Calhoun, a stay-at-home mother, who has lived in Yukon since 2006.<sup id="cite_ref-Yukon2_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Yukon2-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-YNews_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-YNews-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Star_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Star-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> The party began at a meeting in November 2010, and was registered in February 2011.<sup id="cite_ref-WStar_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WStar-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Yukon3_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Yukon3-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Frank De Jong led the party as its interim leader in the <a href="/wiki/Yukon_general_election,_2016" title="Yukon general election, 2016">2016 general election</a>. Its platform in that election included electoral reform, legalizing marijuana, ending public funding for Catholic schools, and introducing a <a href="/wiki/Carbon_tax" title="Carbon tax">carbon tax</a> in Yukon that would be offset by monthly refund payments to Yukoners.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-WDSdj_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WDSdj-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-CBC1_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CBC1-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The party will elect a permanent leader at its annual general meeting in 2017.<sup id="cite_ref-WDSdj_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WDSdj-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The party is in favour of:
</p>
<ul><li>Defunding Catholic schools.<sup id="cite_ref-YUNews_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-YUNews-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup></li>
