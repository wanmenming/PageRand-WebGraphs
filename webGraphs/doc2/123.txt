<p>The <b>Union of Greens of Russia</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Союз зелёных России</span>) or <b>Green Russia</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">Зелёная Россия</span>) was a political party in Russia that was active from 2005 until 2006.
</p><p>In 2005, the organization was founded as an independent party, but in 2006, it merged with the party <a href="/wiki/Yabloko" title="Yabloko">Yabloko</a> as an ecological faction "Green Russia". The "Union of Greens of Russia" and the faction "Green Russia" of Yabloko were headed by professor <a href="/wiki/Alexey_Yablokov" class="mw-redirect" title="Alexey Yablokov">Alexey Yablokov</a>, the founder of <a href="/wiki/Greenpeace" title="Greenpeace">Greenpeace</a> USSR in the 1980s.
</p><p>The faction "Green Russia" is an associate member of the <a href="/wiki/European_Green_Party" title="European Green Party">European Green Party</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
