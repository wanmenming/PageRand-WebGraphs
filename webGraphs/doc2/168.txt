<p>The <b>2018 Green Party of Alberta leadership election</b> winner, <a href="/wiki/Cheryle_Chagnon-Greyeyes" title="Cheryle Chagnon-Greyeyes">Cheryle Chagnon-Greyeyes</a>, was announced on September 22, 2018.<sup id="cite_ref-ABGreens01_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ABGreens01-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Romy_Tittel" title="Romy Tittel">Romy Tittel</a> won the leadership in 2017 and resigned under unknown circumstances less than a year later.<sup id="cite_ref-ABGreens02_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ABGreens02-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The party later named <a href="/w/index.php?title=Coral_Bliss_Taylor&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Coral Bliss Taylor (page does not exist)">Coral Bliss Taylor</a> as interim leader. She is an urban planner and engineer.<sup id="cite_ref-AlbPol01_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AlbPol01-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The vote was determined by <a href="/wiki/Preferential_ballot" class="mw-redirect" title="Preferential ballot">preferential ballot</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ABGreens03_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ABGreens03-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
