<p>The <b>Green Party of Iran</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span lang="fa" dir="rtl">حزب سبز ایران</span>&#8206;, <small><a href="/wiki/Romanization_of_Persian" title="Romanization of Persian">translit.</a>&#160;</small><i lang="fa-Latn" title="Persian-language transliteration">Ḥezb-e Sabz-e Irān</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Iranian_Principlists" title="Iranian Principlists">conservative</a><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> political party in <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iran</a>. The party's founder Hossein Kanani Moghaddam describes it as a <a href="/wiki/Centrist" class="mw-redirect" title="Centrist">centrist</a> party “between the fundamentalists and <a href="/wiki/Iranian_Reformists" title="Iranian Reformists">reformists</a>”.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> As of 2017, two party members Abolfazl Hassanbeigi (Damghan) and Yousef Davoudi (<a href="/wiki/Sarab_(electoral_district)" title="Sarab (electoral district)">Sarab</a>) hold seat in the <a href="/wiki/Iranian_Parliament" class="mw-redirect" title="Iranian Parliament">Iranian Parliament</a>.
</p>
