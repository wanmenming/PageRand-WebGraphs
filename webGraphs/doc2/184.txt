<p>The <b>Green National Committee</b> is the central governing body of the <a href="/wiki/Green_Party_of_the_United_States" title="Green Party of the United States">Green Party of the United States</a>. The GNC is composed of delegates from each of the affiliated state party organizations and from recognized caucuses. The GNC oversees all national party functions and elects a steering committee to oversee day-to-day operations.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Steering_committee">Steering committee</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Green_National_Committee&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Steering committee">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The steering committee is composed of seven co-chairs, acting as a collective executive, together with the secretary and treasurer.  They are elected by the delegates who serve on the Green National Committee.
