<p>The <b>Green Path of Hope</b> (<a href="/wiki/Persian_language" title="Persian language">Persian</a>: <span lang="fa" dir="rtl">شورای هماهنگی راه سبز امید</span>&#8206;)<sup id="cite_ref-BBC_Persian_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBC_Persian-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> is an <a href="/wiki/Iran" title="Iran">Iranian</a> association established by Iranian presidential campaign candidate, <a href="/wiki/Mir-Hossein_Mousavi" title="Mir-Hossein Mousavi">Mir-Hossein Mousavi</a>, on August 15, 2009, as the organizational body of <a href="/wiki/Iranian_Green_Movement" title="Iranian Green Movement">Green Movement</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Mousavi described it as the "countless self-initiated and independent social networks" which form the body of Green Movement.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It has also been mentioned as a "political front" in media.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ilna1_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ilna1-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Name">Name</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Green_Path_of_Hope&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Name">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>According to Mousavi's advisor, Ali Reza Beheshti, Mousavi himself chose the name. He chose "Path", to avoid words such as "party" or "movement", "green", because of the green color symbol used by protesters, and "hope", because he had promised to establish a government of "hope" if elected.<sup id="cite_ref-news.gooya.com_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-news.gooya.com-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Goals">Goals</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Green_Path_of_Hope&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Goals">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Green Path of Hope seeks to continue protests against the legitimacy of Ahmadinejad's presidency following lawful and peaceful ways, and the full execution of the constitution, as Mousavi says:<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

</td>
<td style="vertical-align: top; border: none; padding: 4px 10px;">You can't follow some parts of the constitution and throw the rest into a bin.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Green_Path_of_Hope&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mousavi did not name his movement as a political party or even as a movement, but a "path", because, according to Iranian law, parties and movements need to be authorized by the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Interior_(Iran)" title="Ministry of Interior (Iran)">Interior Ministry</a> and since Mousavi does not recognize the government as legitimate and the ministry is unlikely to grant him permission, he chose this name to bypass the law.<sup id="cite_ref-news.gooya.com_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-news.gooya.com-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Mousavi is quoted in describing the movement:<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<table class="cquote pullquote" role="presentation" style="margin:auto; border-collapse: collapse; border: none; background-color: transparent; width: auto;">
<tbody><tr>
<td style="width: 20px; vertical-align: top; border: none; color: #B2B7F2; font-size: 40px; font-family: &#39;Times New Roman&#39;, Times, serif; font-weight: bold; line-height: .6em; text-align: left; padding: 10px 10px;">“
</td>
<td style="vertical-align: top; border: none; padding: 4px 10px;">The Green Path of Hope is formed for the sake of people’s rightful demands and for claiming their rights... the color green is the symbol of this movement; its slogan is demanding the impeccable implementation of the constitution, and innumerable self-motivated independent societies form the body of this movement.
</td>
<td style="width: 20px; vertical-align: bottom; border: none; color: #B2B7F2; font-size: 40px; font-family: &#39;Times New Roman&#39;, Times, serif; font-weight: bold; line-height: .6em; text-align: right; padding: 10px 10px;">”
</td></tr>

</tbody></table>
<p>According to organization officials, the movement functions on a campaign basis, including <a href="/wiki/Political_parties" class="mw-redirect" title="Political parties">political parties</a>, NGO's and social networks. Referring to the new movement, Mousavi has said "The Green Path of Hope is formed for the sake of people's rightful demands and for claiming their denied rights".<sup id="cite_ref-ilna1_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ilna1-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He pointed that autonomous and spontaneous social networks in community are part of this movement.<sup id="cite_ref-ilna2_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ilna2-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> "During election our mottos chose and remained in constitutional frame work, today we are devoted to those slogans" he said. "We believe if people's demands were treated fairly and instead of using media to link spontaneous movements to foreigners, government promoted truth by fair criticism, they could satisfy public views", he added.<sup id="cite_ref-ilna2_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ilna2-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Methods">Methods</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Green_Path_of_Hope&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Methods">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The way has six main members of the central council, that are connected to the <a href="/wiki/Reformist" class="mw-redirect" title="Reformist">reformist</a> parties and movements, NGOs, and the social networks. The main body will be the ordinary protesters. The method is to create pressure from the lower parts of the society and make them connected in a social network, and therefore to lead the protests in a lawful way.<sup id="cite_ref-news.gooya.com_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-news.gooya.com-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Members_of_the_Central_Council">Members of the Central Council</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=The_Green_Path_of_Hope&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Members of the Central Council">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Green Path of Hope has three central leaders. <a href="/wiki/Hossein_Ali_Montazeri" class="mw-redirect" title="Hossein Ali Montazeri">Hossein Ali Montazeri</a> also had a leading role in the movement in the earlier phase of the movement.
</p>
