<p>The <b>Green Party of Egypt</b> (<i>Hizb Al-khodr</i>) is a <a href="/wiki/Green_party" title="Green party">Green political party</a> in <a href="/wiki/Egypt" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>. The party presses for protection and promotion of the ecological system and optimal use of resources. It also calls for drawing up solutions to the problems of poverty, underdevelopment, and challenges the disadvantages of globalism and capitalism.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Egyptian_Green_Party&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was founded by former diplomat Hassan Ragab in 1990, experienced a hiatus in 1995, and was revived in 1998. In the internal party elections in 2000 Dr. Abdel Munem Ali Ali Al Aasar was elected president of the Green Party.<sup id="cite_ref-ad_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ad-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He was later appointed to the <a href="/wiki/Shura_Council" title="Shura Council">Shura Council</a> (the upper House of Egypt's parliament).<sup id="cite_ref-ad_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ad-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> However, political activities became increasingly difficult in the final years of the Mubarak regime and the party remained ineffective.<sup id="cite_ref-ei_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ei-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There was a revival in the aftermath of the <a href="/wiki/Arab_Spring" title="Arab Spring">Arab Spring</a> in 2011, and the party fielded candidates in the <a href="/wiki/Egyptian_parliamentary_election,_2011%E2%80%9312" title="Egyptian parliamentary election, 2011–12">2012 elections</a>.
</p><p>The party is a member of the <a href="/wiki/Global_Greens" title="Global Greens">Global Greens</a> and the African Greens.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
