<p>The <b>Uganda Green Party</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Political_party" title="Political party">political party</a> in <a href="/wiki/Uganda" title="Uganda">Uganda</a>. The party claims to be committed to the well-being of the <a href="/wiki/Natural_environment" title="Natural environment">natural environment</a> rather than to politics. Its main objective is to protect the environment and its habitats.
</p>
