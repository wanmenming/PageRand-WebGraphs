<p>The <b>Somalia Green Party</b> (<b>SGP</b>) is the local <a href="/wiki/Green_party" title="Green party">green party</a> of <a href="/wiki/Somalia" title="Somalia">Somalia</a>. It has an <a href="/wiki/Environmentalism" title="Environmentalism">environmentalist</a> focus. The SGP is a member of the Horn of African Greens in the <a href="/wiki/Federation_of_Green_Parties_of_Africa" title="Federation of Green Parties of Africa">Federation of Green Parties of Africa</a>, which includes the national green parties in the other <a href="/wiki/Horn_of_Africa" title="Horn of Africa">Horn of Africa</a> states of <a href="/wiki/Djibouti" title="Djibouti">Djibouti</a>, <a href="/wiki/Eritrea" title="Eritrea">Eritrea</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ethiopia" title="Ethiopia">Ethiopia</a>. The association has its headquarters in <a href="/wiki/Mogadishu" title="Mogadishu">Mogadishu</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jilib" title="Jilib">Jilib</a> in southern Somalia. Plans are also in the works to open up new SGP branches in the autonomous northern <a href="/wiki/Puntland" title="Puntland">Puntland</a> and <a href="/wiki/Somaliland" title="Somaliland">Somaliland</a> regions, as well as the southernmost <a href="/wiki/Kismayo" title="Kismayo">Kismayo</a> area. Additionally, the party has its international and communications office in <a href="/wiki/Ottawa,_Ontario" class="mw-redirect" title="Ottawa, Ontario">Ottawa, Ontario</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>.
</p>
