<p>The <b>Green Party of Prince Edward Island</b> leadership election took place on 2–3 November, <a href="/wiki/Prince_Edward_Island" title="Prince Edward Island">Prince Edward Island</a>.
</p><p>On 12 July 2012, <a href="/wiki/Sharon_Labchuk" title="Sharon Labchuk">Sharon Labchuk</a> resigned from the leadership of the <a href="/wiki/Green_Party_of_PEI" class="mw-redirect" title="Green Party of PEI">Green Party of PEI</a>, triggering a leadership election. An interim leader and the details of the leadership election are to be announced.<sup id="cite_ref-TGC_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-TGC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On 3 November 2012, <a href="/wiki/Peter_Bevan-Baker" title="Peter Bevan-Baker">Peter Bevan-Baker</a> was elected leader.<sup id="cite_ref-theguardian.pe.ca_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-theguardian.pe.ca-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
