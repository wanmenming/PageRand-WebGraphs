<p><b>Midnight green</b> (sometimes called <b>Eagles green</b><sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2014)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>) is a dark shade of <a href="/wiki/Teal" title="Teal">teal</a>. This can be readily ascertained by noting the fact that this color's red value is zero, and also that its green and blue values are almost equal.
</p>
