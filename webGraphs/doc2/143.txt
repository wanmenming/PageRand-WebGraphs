<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">List of green political parties</h1>			<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
				<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>				<div id="contentSub"><span class="mw-redirectedfrom">  (Redirected from <a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_green_parties&amp;redirect=no" class="mw-redirect" title="List of green parties">List of green parties</a>)</span></div>
				<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>This is a list of parties in the world that consider themselves to be upholding the principles and values of <a href="/wiki/Green_politics" title="Green politics">green politics</a>. Some are also members of the <a href="/wiki/Global_Greens" title="Global Greens">Global Greens</a>, the <a href="/wiki/European_Green_Party" title="European Green Party">European Green Party</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Nordic_Green_Left_Alliance" title="Nordic Green Left Alliance">Nordic Green Left Alliance</a> or other international organizations. Note that, in some cases, a party's self-described adherence to environmentalism may be disputed by its critics.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="I">I</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=List_of_green_political_parties&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: I">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<dl><dt>India</dt></dl>
<ul><li><a href="/w/index.php?title=Uttarakhand_Parivartan_Party_(UKPP)&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Uttarakhand Parivartan Party (UKPP) (page does not exist)">Uttarakhand Parivartan Party (UKPP)</a></li></ul>
<p>The Uttarakhand Parivartan Party (UKPP) - directly translated in English as the "Uttarakhand Transformation Party", is the first Green party in India and is registered in the state of Uttarakhand.
UKPP was formed on January 18, 2009 after two years of grassroots deliberations.  The party fielded two candidates in the 2009 parliamentary elections and 15 candidates in the 2012 state assembly elections.  UKPP is raising awareness about Green politics among the masses, although yet to win representation in government.
</p>
