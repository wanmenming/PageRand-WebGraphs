<p>The <b>Civil United Green Alternative movement</b>, <b>GROZA movement</b> (<a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">движение Гражданская Объединённая Зелёная Альтернатива</span>, <a href="/wiki/Russian_language" title="Russian language">Russian</a>: <span lang="ru">движение ГРОЗА</span>), formerly <b>Interregional Green Party</b>, is a <a href="/wiki/Politics_of_Russia" title="Politics of Russia">Russian</a> movement, founded in 1991 as a local party in <a href="/wiki/Leningrad" class="mw-redirect" title="Leningrad">Leningrad</a>. In 2005, it reorganized in the green political movement "Green Alternative" (GROZA). The movement is an associate member of the <a href="/wiki/European_Green_Party" title="European Green Party">European Green Party</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
