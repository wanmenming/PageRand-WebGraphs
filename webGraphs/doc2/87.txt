<p>The <b>Nordic Green Left Alliance</b> (<b>NGLA</b>) is an alliance of <a href="/wiki/Nordic_countries" title="Nordic countries">Nordic</a> <a href="/wiki/Left-wing_politics" title="Left-wing politics">left-wing</a> parties, founded in <a href="/wiki/Reykjav%C3%ADk" title="Reykjavík">Reykjavík</a>, <a href="/wiki/Iceland" title="Iceland">Iceland</a>, on 1 February 2004. In 2018, the intra-EU parties of the alliance joined <a href="/wiki/Maintenant_le_Peuple" title="Maintenant le Peuple">Maintenant le Peuple</a>.
</p>

<p>Most of the member parties of NGLA have not joined the <a href="/wiki/Party_of_the_European_Left" title="Party of the European Left">Party of the European Left</a> (the Finnish Left Alliance being a recent exception), but NGLA participates in EL events as an observer. In the <a href="/wiki/European_Parliament" title="European Parliament">European Parliament</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Member_of_the_European_Parliament" title="Member of the European Parliament">MEPs</a> of the Swedish Left Party and of the Finnish Left Alliance are part of the <a href="/wiki/European_United_Left%E2%80%93Nordic_Green_Left" title="European United Left–Nordic Green Left">European United Left–Nordic Green Left</a> parliamentary group, whilst the Danish Socialist People's Party MEP is member of the <a href="/wiki/Greens-EFA" class="mw-redirect" title="Greens-EFA">Greens-EFA</a> group.
