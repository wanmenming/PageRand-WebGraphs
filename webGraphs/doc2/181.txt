<p>The <b>Green Party</b> (<a href="/wiki/German_language" title="German language">German</a>: <i lang="de">Grüne Partei</i>) in <a href="/wiki/East_Germany" title="East Germany">East Germany</a> was founded in February 1990. At the first free <a href="/wiki/Volkskammer" title="Volkskammer">Volkskammer</a> elections it stood with the <a href="/wiki/Independent_Women%27s_Association" title="Independent Women&#39;s Association">Independent Women's Association</a> (<i lang="de" title="German language text">Unabhängige Frauenverband</i>) and received 2.0% of the vote. They received 8 seats but could not resolve how to allocate them. The Women's Association pulled out and the Green Party formed a joint parliamentary group with <a href="/wiki/B%C3%BCndnis_90" class="mw-redirect" title="Bündnis 90">Bündnis 90</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
