<p>The <b>Green Party of South Africa</b> (<b>GPSA</b>) is a small <a href="/wiki/Political_party" title="Political party">political party</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Western_Cape_Province" class="mw-redirect" title="Western Cape Province">Western Cape</a> <a href="/wiki/Province" title="Province">province</a>.  It has similar policies to <a href="/wiki/Worldwide_green_parties" class="mw-redirect" title="Worldwide green parties">Green parties</a> around the world, and is a member of the <a href="/wiki/Federation_of_Green_Parties_of_Africa" title="Federation of Green Parties of Africa">Federation of Green Parties of Africa</a>.
</p>

<p><b>The Ecology Party</b> was established in November 1989, but disbanded after recruiting only 1&#160;800 members.<sup id="cite_ref-kotze1994_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-kotze1994-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ian Brownlie launched the <b>Green Party</b> (<b>GRP</b>) in July 1992, but it too disbanded after failing to win a seat in the 1994 Western Cape provincial <a href="/wiki/Election" title="Election">elections</a> under the leadership of <a href="/w/index.php?title=Nathan_Grant&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Nathan Grant (page does not exist)">Nathan Grant</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-kotze1994_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-kotze1994-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/w/index.php?title=Judy_Sole&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Judy Sole (page does not exist)">Judy Sole</a> founded the <b>Government by the People Green Party</b> (<b>GPGP</b>) in 1999, and the party has contested elections as the Green Party of South Africa since the 2000 local elections.
</p>
