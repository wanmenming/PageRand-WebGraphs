<p><b>Green Zionism</b> is a branch of <a href="/wiki/Zionism" title="Zionism">Zionism</a> that is primarily concerned with the environment of <a href="/wiki/Israel" title="Israel">Israel</a>. It mostly fuses Israeli-specific environmental concerns with support for the existence of Israel as a <a href="/wiki/Jewish_state" title="Jewish state">Jewish state</a>. The term itself is a trademark of <a href="/wiki/Aytzim" title="Aytzim">Aytzim</a>, the first <a href="/wiki/Environmentalism" title="Environmentalism">environmental</a> organization to participate in the <a href="/wiki/World_Zionist_Congress" title="World Zionist Congress">World Zionist Congress</a>, the <a href="/wiki/World_Zionist_Organization" title="World Zionist Organization">World Zionist Organization</a>, and its constituent agencies.<sup id="cite_ref-Forward_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Forward-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
