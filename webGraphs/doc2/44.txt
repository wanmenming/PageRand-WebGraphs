<p><b>Green Italy</b> (<a href="/wiki/Italian_language" title="Italian language">Italian</a>: <i lang="it">Green Italia</i>, GI) is a <a href="/wiki/Green_politics" title="Green politics">green</a> <a href="/wiki/List_of_political_parties_in_Italy" title="List of political parties in Italy">political party in Italy</a>.
</p><p>The party was founded in June 2013 by a heterogeneous group of politicians, notably including <a href="/wiki/Monica_Frassoni" title="Monica Frassoni">Monica Frassoni</a> (co-spokesperson of the <a href="/wiki/European_Green_Party" title="European Green Party">European Green Party</a> and former independent MEP of the <a href="/wiki/Federation_of_the_Greens" title="Federation of the Greens">Federation of the Greens</a>), <a href="/w/index.php?title=Fabio_Granata&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Fabio Granata (page does not exist)">Fabio Granata</a> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Flavia_Perina&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Flavia Perina (page does not exist)">Flavia Perina</a> (former members and deputies of the <a href="/wiki/Italian_Social_Movement" title="Italian Social Movement">Italian Social Movement</a>, <a href="/wiki/National_Alliance_(Italy)" title="National Alliance (Italy)">National Alliance</a>, <a href="/wiki/The_People_of_Freedom" title="The People of Freedom">The People of Freedom</a> and <a href="/wiki/Future_and_Freedom" title="Future and Freedom">Future and Freedom</a>), <a href="/w/index.php?title=Roberto_Della_Seta&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Roberto Della Seta (page does not exist)">Roberto Della Seta</a> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Francesco_Ferrante&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Francesco Ferrante (page does not exist)">Francesco Ferrante</a> (former senators of the <a href="/wiki/Democratic_Party_(Italy)" title="Democratic Party (Italy)">Democratic Party</a> and former leaders of <i><a href="/wiki/Legambiente" title="Legambiente">Legambiente</a></i>), <a href="/w/index.php?title=Marco_Boato&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Marco Boato (page does not exist)">Marco Boato</a>, <a href="/w/index.php?title=Fiorello_Cortiana&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Fiorello Cortiana (page does not exist)">Fiorello Cortiana</a> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Anna_Donati&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Anna Donati (page does not exist)">Anna Donati</a> (former Green MPs; Boato, a former <a href="/wiki/Radical_Party_(Italy)" title="Radical Party (Italy)">Radical</a>, is still an active member of that party), as well as <a href="/wiki/Green_economy" title="Green economy">green economy</a> entrepreneurs, intellectuals and activists.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-chisiamo_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-chisiamo-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>For the <a href="/wiki/European_Parliament_election,_2014_(Italy)" title="European Parliament election, 2014 (Italy)">2014 European Parliament election</a> the party presented joint lists with the Greens, under the name <a href="/wiki/Green_Italy_%E2%80%93_European_Greens" title="Green Italy – European Greens">Green Italy – European Greens</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> The list obtained a mere 0.9% of the vote—and 6.0% among Italians abroad.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In the municipal election of <a href="/wiki/Padua" title="Padua">Padua</a> <a href="/w/index.php?title=Francesco_Fiore&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Francesco Fiore (page does not exist)">Francesco Fiore</a>, a member of GI's national board,<sup id="cite_ref-chisiamo_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-chisiamo-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> came fourth with 9.9% at the head of a civic list.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
