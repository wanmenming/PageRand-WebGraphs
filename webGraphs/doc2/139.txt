<p>The <b>Green Party of Malaysia</b> or Parti Hijau Malaysia is a political party in <a href="/wiki/Malaysia" title="Malaysia">Malaysia</a> with an <a href="/wiki/Environmentalism" title="Environmentalism">environmentalism</a> focus. Founder <a href="/w/index.php?title=Azlan_Adnan&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Azlan Adnan (page does not exist)">Azlan Adnan</a>'s original intent was to empower and unite all Environmental NGOs (ENGOs) in Malaysia and to push the green agenda into the Malaysian political consciousness.
</p>
