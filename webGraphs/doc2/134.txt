<p><b>Green municipalism</b> refers to the encouragement of <a href="/wiki/Environmentalism" title="Environmentalism">environmentalism</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Municipality" title="Municipality">municipal</a>, rather than state or national basis.
</p><p>It has been discussed by <a href="/w/index.php?title=Brian_Milani&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Brian Milani (page does not exist)">Brian Milani</a> in his book, <i><a href="/w/index.php?title=Designing_the_Green_Economy&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Designing the Green Economy (page does not exist)">Designing the Green Economy</a></i> (2000).<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and by the <a href="/wiki/Syracuse,_New_York" title="Syracuse, New York">Syracuse</a> / Onondaga County Green Party Chapter at the US National Green Gathering of August 1992.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
