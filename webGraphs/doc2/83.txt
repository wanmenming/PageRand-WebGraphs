<p>The <b>Pakistan Green Party</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Green_politics" title="Green politics">green</a> democratic <a href="/wiki/Political_party" title="Political party">political party</a> in Pakistan. It was founded on April 28, 2002,<sup id="cite_ref-Miranda_Schreurs_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Miranda_Schreurs-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and since has been active in local politics. Members of the political party are known as the "<b>Pakistan Greens</b>". <a href="/w/index.php?title=Liaquat_Ali_Shaikh&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Liaquat Ali Shaikh (page does not exist)">Liaquat Ali Shaikh</a> heads the legal party.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="10_basic_values">10 basic values</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Green_Party_of_Pakistan&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: 10 basic values">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The ten basic values, or policy positions of the Pakistan Greens are similar to the ten principles of other green parties<i>:</i>
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Grassroots_democracy" title="Grassroots democracy">Grassroots democracy</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Social" title="Social">Social</a> <a href="/wiki/Justice" title="Justice">Justice</a> and <a href="/wiki/Equal_opportunity" title="Equal opportunity">Equal opportunity</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Ecological_wisdom" class="mw-redirect" title="Ecological wisdom">Ecological wisdom</a></li>
