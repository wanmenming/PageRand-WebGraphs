<p>The <b>Ecologist Green Party</b> (<a href="/wiki/Romanian_language" title="Romanian language">Romanian</a>: <i lang="ro">Partidul Verde Ecologist</i>), formerly known as the Ecologist Party of Moldova "Green Alliance" (<a href="/wiki/Romanian_language" title="Romanian language">Romanian</a>: <i lang="ro">Partidul Ecologist din Moldova "Alianţa Verde"</i>, PEMAV) is a <a href="/wiki/Green_politics" title="Green politics">green</a> <a href="/wiki/Political_party" title="Political party">political party</a> in <a href="/wiki/Moldova" title="Moldova">Moldova</a>.
</p>

<p>The party was established on April 9, 1992. The Founding Congress adopted the program and bylaws and elected the governing bodies. <a href="/wiki/Gheorghe_Malarciuc" title="Gheorghe Malarciuc">Gheorghe Malarciuc</a> was the first leader of the party.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
