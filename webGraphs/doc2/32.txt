<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" lang="en">Outline of green politics</h1>			<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">
				<div id="siteSub" class="noprint">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</div>				<div id="contentSub"></div>
				<div id="jump-to-nav"></div>				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#mw-head">Jump to navigation</a>
				<a class="mw-jump-link" href="#p-search">Jump to search</a>
				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The following <a href="/wiki/Outline_(list)" title="Outline (list)">outline</a> is provided as an overview and topical guide to green politics:
</p><p><b><a href="/wiki/Green_politics" title="Green politics">Green politics</a></b> &#8211; <a href="/wiki/Political_ideology" class="mw-redirect" title="Political ideology">political ideology</a> that aims for the creation of an ecologically sustainable society rooted in <a href="/wiki/Environmentalism" title="Environmentalism">environmentalism</a>, <a href="/wiki/Social_liberalism" title="Social liberalism">social liberalism</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Grassroots_democracy" title="Grassroots democracy">grassroots democracy</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Wall_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Wall-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  It began taking shape in the western world in the 1970s; since then Green parties have developed and established themselves in many countries across the globe, and have achieved some electoral success.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Policy_issues">Policy issues</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Outline_of_green_politics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Policy issues">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A few issues affect most of the green parties around the world, and can often inhibit global cooperation. Some affect structure, and others affect policy:
</p>
<ul><li><a href="/wiki/Anti-nuclear" class="mw-redirect" title="Anti-nuclear">Anti-nuclear</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bioregionalism" title="Bioregionalism">Bioregionalism</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Biosafety" title="Biosafety">Biosafety</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Biosecurity" title="Biosecurity">Biosecurity</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Electoral_reform" title="Electoral reform">Electoral reform</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Fundi_(politics)" title="Fundi (politics)">Fundamentalism</a> vs. <a href="/wiki/Realism_(international_relations)" title="Realism (international relations)">Realism</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Greenwashing" title="Greenwashing">Greenwashing</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Indigenous_peoples" title="Indigenous peoples">Indigenous peoples</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Land_reform" title="Land reform">Land reform</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Natural_capitalism" class="mw-redirect" title="Natural capitalism">Natural capitalism</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Primate_extinction" class="mw-redirect" title="Primate extinction">Primate extinction</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Prometheanism" title="Prometheanism">Prometheanism</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Rainforest_destruction" class="mw-redirect" title="Rainforest destruction">Rainforest destruction</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Safe_trade" title="Safe trade">Safe trade</a></li></ul>
<p>On matters of <a href="/wiki/Ecology" title="Ecology">ecology</a>, <a href="/wiki/Extinction" title="Extinction">extinction</a>, <a href="/wiki/Biosafety" title="Biosafety">biosafety</a>, <a href="/wiki/Biosecurity" title="Biosecurity">biosecurity</a>, <a href="/wiki/Safe_trade" title="Safe trade">safe trade</a> and <a href="/w/index.php?title=Health_security&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Health security (page does not exist)">health security</a>, "<a href="/wiki/Green_party" title="Green party">Greens</a>" generally agree.  There are very substantial policy differences between and among Green Parties in various countries and cultures, and a continuing debate about the degree to which natural ecology and human needs align.  Agreement on particular issues is often reached using a <a href="/wiki/Consensus_decision-making" title="Consensus decision-making">consensus decision making</a> process.
</p>
